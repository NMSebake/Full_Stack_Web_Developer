Marinated overnight, the chicken turns out tender, juicy, and irresistibly delicious â€” the kind of dish that will have everyone begging for the recipe after just one bite.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 cup cider vineger
  • 3 tablespoons ground mustard
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 6 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 skinless, boneless chicken breasts halves

Steps
  1. Mix cider vinegar, mustard, garlic, lime juice, lemon juice, brown sugar, salt, and
    pepper together in a large glass or ceramic bowl. Whisk in olive oil. Add chicken
    and toss evenly to coat. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator for 8 hours to
    overnight.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  3. Lightly oil the grill grate. Place chicken on the preheated grill, and cook 6 to 8
    minutes per side, until juices run clear. Discard any remaining marinade.

Tomato Pie

Description

 Bursting with fresh summer flavor, this Tomato Pie layers juicy ripe tomatoes, smoky bacon, fresh basil, and
melty cheddar in a flaky crust for the ultimate savory treat. Creamy, cheesy, and perfectly seasoned, it’s a
Southern classic that turns simple garden ingredients into a show-stopping dish everyone will beg you to
make again.

Ingredients
  • 1 (9-inch) deep dish pie crust
  • 4 large tomatoes, peeled and sliced
  • ½ pound bacon - cooked, drained, and chopped
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • ¼ cup mayonnaise

Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In alternating layers, fill pie crust with tomatoes, bacon, green onions, basil,
    oregano, garlic powder, and red pepper.
  3. Stir together cheese and mayonnaise in a small bowl.
  4. Spread over the top of pie. Cover loosely with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking for 30 more minutes.
  6. Serve pie warm or cold.
